For decades, the concept of artificial intelligence was confined to the pages of science fiction. Today, it is the silent engine driving the modern world. At the heart of this revolution is Machine Learning (ML), a subset of AI focused on building systems that learn from data rather than following rigid, pre-programmed rules. In recent years, a perfect storm of massive data availability, specialized hardware, and algorithmic breakthroughs has catapulted ML from a niche academic pursuit to a foundational pillar of global industry.
